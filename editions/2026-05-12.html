Deutsch

English

Google DeepMind has unveiled an AI co-mathematician that scored a new high of 48% on the FrontierMath Tier 4 benchmark — roughly double the previous best. The multi-agent system, built on Gemini 3.1, tackles open math problems with a division of labor: a coordinator agent directing sub-agents for literature search, code writing, and proof attempts. Oxford mathematician Marc Lackenby used it to resolve an open problem from the Kourovka Notebook — a reviewer agent spotted a flaw in an earlier proof attempt that Lackenby himself had missed.
